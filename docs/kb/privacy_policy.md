# Privacy Policy

## Data We Collect
- Email address (for account creation and communication)
- Audio recordings (for pronunciation analysis)
- Analysis results (scores, transcripts, phoneme data)
- Anonymous usage data (session identifiers, analysis count)

## How We Use Your Data
- Audio recordings are processed solely for pronunciation analysis using Deepgram speech recognition technology.
- Your recordings are NEVER sent to third-party AI services. Only text-based phonetic metadata is sent to our LLM provider (Gemini via OpenRouter) for generating feedback explanations.
- We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

## Data Retention
- Raw audio files are automatically deleted after 30 days.
- Derived data (scores, phoneme analysis, practice sets) is retained as long as your account exists to provide progress tracking.
- Anonymous session data is retained for 30 days.

## Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)
- **Right to Access**: View all your data from the "My Data" settings page.
- **Right to Erasure**: Delete your account and all associated data at any time.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: You can withdraw consent for audio processing, which stops further analysis until re-consented.

## Consent
Before uploading any audio, you must explicitly consent to:
1. Audio processing for pronunciation analysis
2. Data retention as described above
3. Acceptance of this privacy policy

Consent is recorded with a timestamp and policy version for audit purposes.

## Data Security
- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
- Passwords are hashed using Argon2 (industry standard).
- Audio files are stored with random UUID filenames (not user-identifiable).
- Access tokens expire after 15 minutes; refresh tokens after 7 days.

## Contact
For privacy-related questions, contact our data protection team through the in-app assistant.
