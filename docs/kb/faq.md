# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is AccentIQ?
AccentIQ is an AI-powered tool that analyzes your spoken English and provides personalized feedback to help you improve your pronunciation. It uses advanced speech recognition technology to identify specific sounds you might be struggling with.

## How do I use it?
1. Upload an audio recording of yourself speaking English (15-45 seconds).
2. Wait while the system analyzes your pronunciation.
3. View your score and see which words were flagged.
4. Click on any highlighted word to get a detailed explanation of how to improve.
5. Practice with personalized sentences targeting your weak sounds.

## Do I need to register?
You can try 3 free analyses without registering. After that, you'll need to create an account with your email. Registration is free and gives you unlimited analyses plus progress tracking.

## What audio formats are supported?
We accept WAV, MP3, MP4/M4A, OGG, FLAC, and WebM audio files. You can also record directly in your browser using your microphone.

## How long should my recording be?
Your recording should be between 15 and 45 seconds. Shorter recordings don't provide enough data for accurate analysis, and longer ones exceed our processing limits.

## Is my audio data safe?
Yes. Your audio is processed on our servers and is never shared with third parties. Raw audio files are automatically deleted after 30 days. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

## Can I delete my data?
Yes. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings page. This removes all recordings, transcripts, scores, and personal information permanently.
