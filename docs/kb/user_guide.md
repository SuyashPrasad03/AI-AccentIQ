# User Guide

## Getting Started
1. Visit the app and accept the privacy consent.
2. Upload an audio recording or record directly in your browser.
3. Wait for processing (usually 15-30 seconds).
4. View your results — words are color-coded by quality.

## Recording Tips
- Find a quiet environment with minimal background noise.
- Speak clearly at a natural pace — don't rush or over-enunciate.
- Hold your device 6-12 inches from your mouth.
- Record between 15 and 45 seconds of continuous speech.
- Reading a paragraph aloud works well for consistent results.

## Understanding Your Results

### Score Card
The main score (0-100) shows your overall pronunciation quality. Sub-scores show Accuracy (phoneme precision) and Fluency (pace and rhythm).

### Color-Coded Words
- **Green**: Correctly pronounced.
- **Red**: Mispronounced — a specific sound substitution detected.
- **Yellow/Orange**: Unclear — spoken too quickly or too softly.
- **Blue**: Mistimed — unusual word duration.

### Click to Explain
Click any non-green word to get:
- An explanation of what went wrong.
- A tip on mouth/tongue positioning.
- Practice words using the same sound.

## Practice Sentences
After analysis, you'll see personalized practice sentences targeting your specific weak sounds. These are regenerated daily and use words that heavily feature the sounds you need to practice.

## Progress Tracking (Registered Users)
After your second recording, you'll see comparison cards showing how your scores changed:
- Overall Score delta (e.g., 76 → 84, +8)
- Per-phoneme improvements (e.g., TH: 61 → 88)
- A history chart showing your trend over time.

## Account Management
- **Register**: Provide your email, verify with a 6-digit OTP code, set a password.
- **Sign in**: Email + password. Sessions last 7 days.
- **Delete account**: Settings → "Delete my account and all data."
